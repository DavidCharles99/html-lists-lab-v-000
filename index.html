• 2 slices of bread
  • 4 slices of cheese
    • cheddar
    • mozzarella
    • pepper jack
  • 1 tbsp of butter

Making A Grilled Cheese Sandwich

  1. Put butter on bread and frying pan,
  2. Lay bread in pan and heat,
  3. Place cheese on top of the bread,
  4. Cover with second slice of bread,
  5. Turn over and heat for 2 minutes
